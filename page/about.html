Promoting entrepreneurship, innovation and vision is our goal and we do it through start up incubation and angel investment. The team at Be Yourself Ventures looks out for potential entrepreneurs, courageous founders and disruptive start-ups that the team believes has the potential to be a globally recognized company and that can disrupt the industry it is in and will significantly contribute to our lives and the way we do things.

We look and search for these potential start ups, be it in IT and technology driven industry or non IT based industry and we listen to their ideas, their business plan and their vision and we help them by nurturing from the start with mentoring and strategic guidance so that they can expand and scale their business and disrupt the industry they are in by innovating their journey towards their vision.

We start with angel investing in those start ups and we stay with them like angels to guide them throughout their journey towards their goal and vision.

What we do

Most of the start-ups in our country cannot scale due to the lack of proper guidance and financial support. We at Be Yourself Ventures assess the business model and plan of the potential start-up and if it matches with our policy/guideline then we join hands with them as Angels and provide them with Angel Investment. Not only investment we also help these start-ups with proper mentoring, guidance and strategic support to expand and grow their business. Like an Angel we are always with them throughout their journey towards their vision.

How we do it

An elite board comprising of experts and industry professionals at “Be Yourself Ventures Limited” will take the final decision for business idea and start up selection for Angel Investment. Potential Entrepreneurs and founders will present their business plan and vision with the team of Be Yourself Ventures who will evaluate the plan and will recommend the board about these start ups and the final decision rests with the board about whether to go ahead with Investment in those start ups or not. Once decided then an agreement will be signed between Be Yourself Ventures & the founder and their company after all the terms and conditions/policies are agreed upon by both the parties.
